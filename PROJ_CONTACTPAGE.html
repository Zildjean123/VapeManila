About Us

Friends committed to find a better alternative to smoking helped us discover vaping. With vaping, we had great products at the time but supplies of quality e-liquids were limited in our local market. We decided to bring our most loved, uniquely gratifying and delightful liquids first at our doorstep and now to yours. Our mission is to provide our community with high quality, personally tested goods that we think you will love like we do. A variety of flavors to choose from that best suited one's palate and taste. Of course, our company mandate is to offer the best service possible to all our clients. Without you, we are nothing.
